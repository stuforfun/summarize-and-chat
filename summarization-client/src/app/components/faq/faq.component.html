Frequently Asked Questions:

Q :What types of documents are supported by vSummarizer? A :Summarizer supports PDF, Microsoft Word, PowerPoint, text and popular audio file types (mp3, mp4, mpeg, mpga, m4a, wav, and webm) and Web Video Text Tracks (VTT) file type. Q :What is the size limit for a document? A :Files up to 500MB can be uploaded through the "Upload Doc" button. Q :How do I generate a text summary? A :You can insert, paste or upload your text file, use the QUICK executive summary template or select the DETAILED option to select the model, write your prompts, set the chunk size, chunk overlap, summary type & summary length. Then click the Generate Summary button. For longer text, you will see the estimated time to finish and receive email notification when it is done.
Q :How do I generate a meeting recording summary? A :You first upload your audio file. For large audio files, you will receive email notification after the speech-to-text conversion is done. Click the link in the email or go to My Files page and select the file you just uploaded, then click the Summarize button from the dropdown. Follow the steps in "How do I generate a text summary?".
Q :How do I chat with my document? A :In the Summarizer page, you can click the Intelligent Assist icon on the top menu to start chatting with your document as shown pic 1. You can pick one of the auto-generated questions or enter your own question and get the answer with the source in seconds. In the My Files page, select the document that you want to chat and click the Chat button from the dropdown to chat with your document.
